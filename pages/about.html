About Tier 1 Home Services

Your Trusted Partner in Home Transformation Since 2008

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Tier 1 Home Services has been transforming homes across the DFW area for over 15 years. What started as a small family-owned business has grown into one of the most trusted names in home remodeling and renovation.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide homeowners with exceptional quality workmanship, honest pricing, and unparalleled customer service. Today, we continue to uphold these values in every project we undertake, from small bathroom updates to complete home renovations.

Our Mission

At Tier 1 Home Services, our mission is to turn your house into the home of your dreams. We believe that every homeowner deserves a beautiful, functional living space that reflects their unique style and meets their specific needs.

We achieve this by combining skilled craftsmanship, premium materials, innovative design solutions, and a commitment to excellence that shows in every detail. Your satisfaction is our success, and we won't rest until you're absolutely thrilled with the results.
